hair save

dry shampoo

for all hair types

4 oz/118 ml

An easy to use dry shampoo that absorbs oil and removes impurities without water. All natural and safe! It can be used on clean hair as well as unwashed hair for more volume and texture.

Directions: Sprinkle a small amount into palm, and place small pinches all around the scalp. Massage in well, and brush out any excess.

Ingredients: arrowroot powder, neem powder*, rosemary oil*.

*Organic
