for the body

deodorant

for all skin types

2 oz/60 g

Here is a natural deodorant you can rely on. It lasts all day, and is safe for you and your family. It is paraben free and aluminum free. Note: when switching to a natural deodorant, it can take up to two weeks to detoxify the aluminum and other chemicals that have been stored in the body, so don’t be discouraged! Your body’s health is worth it.

Directions: Apply a small amount to clean underarms. Tip: if the product is super hard during the winter, hold your arm down over your hand for 10 seconds before rubbing it in. That will allow it to warm up and glide on smoothly. After sweating, may need to reapply, like with all natural deodorants.

Ingredients: Arrowroot powder, coconut oil*, baking soda, shea butter*, sandalwood oil*.

*Organic
