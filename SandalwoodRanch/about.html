about

My name is Shayna Blackmar, and I am the creator of Sandalwood Ranch. I have been an esthetician for 9+ years, and I have seen and treated all types of skin issues during that time. Not only am I passionate about skin, I am also passionate about overall body health. I have seen how the body's good or bad health affects our skin, and how good habits and products make a huge difference. I have experienced it myself having had adult acne throughout college and my early 20's. Through my own personal journey, I have discovered that the best skincare takes a two step approach of both internal care and external care.

When I discovered Ayurveda, it was like coming home. Ayurveda is all about finding balance through the connection of the inside and the outside, whether it is the skin or the earth we are talking about. Everything is connected, and we strive to do the best for both the skin, the earth and everything in between here at Sandalwood Ranch. All our ingredients are the best we can find: organic, natural, and sustainable. The packaging is sustainable as well. There is not now and never will be any harsh chemicals or additives.

Combining skincare and Ayurveda just makes sense, and we will help your skin find its balance for optimal health and radiance.
